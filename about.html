About Us

關於我們

中國香港硬地滾球總會致力推廣「硬地滾球」普及化，讓更多市民接觸並參與其中，感受這項運動的樂趣。

The Boccia Association of Hong Kong, China Limited is committed to promoting boccia participation and helping more people experience the enjoyment of the sport.

Our Focus

讓硬地滾球走進更多場景

我們相信硬地滾球既是運動，也是連結社區與建立自信的平台。透過推廣、培訓及器材支援，本會希望讓不同年齡、不同能力人士都有機會參與。

We see boccia as both a sport and a platform for community connection. Through outreach, training, and equipment support, we aim to make participation more accessible.

What We Provide

多元化服務

A

推廣活動

與非牟利機構、學校及企業合作，策劃體驗活動、示範及推廣項目。

Partnership programmes with NGOs, schools, and companies, including demonstrations and participation sessions.

B

教練及裁判訓練班

培育更多專業人才，推動訓練水平，並保持比賽質素與公平性。

Coach and referee training to strengthen professional standards and competition integrity.

C

租借或購買球具套裝

協助機構、學校及活動單位安排合適球具，讓活動更容易啟動。

Boccia set rental or purchase support for organisations, schools, and event organisers.
